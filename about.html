palette

The background color used all all pages on this site is Cosmic Latte - the average color of the universe. Cosmic Latte was discovered by astronomers Karl Glazebrook and Ivan Baldry, initially in 2002, then corrected in 2003. Its hex triplet is #FFF8E7.

The detailing throughout the site consists primarily of three colors: Aqua Sky (#7BC4C4), Living Coral (#FF6F61), and Very Peri (#6667AB). These three colors were the Pantone color of the year in 2003, 2019, and 2022, respectively.

These colors are subject to change regularly, depending on mood.

style
